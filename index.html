Centre Overview

Amazing rooftop playground An oasis for children in the middle of Melbourne CBD
Exceeding NQS Outstanding quality in a boutique setting
Established service With 16 years (and counting) serving the community and a loyal, long-serving team

Sentia Early Learning is a one of a kind child care centre on Flinders Street in the heart of Melbourne’s CBD.

The team is committed to building strong relationships with the children and families, and their high staff retention is a key indicator of happy educators at the centre. Sentia Early Learning has an atmosphere of safety and joy, crafted to inspire children, build their confidence and take their first steps toward a lifelong love of learning.
